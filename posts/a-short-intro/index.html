A Short Intro

28 April 2018

Hello there! I wasn't sure whether to have any introductory post at all. Information and content matter most, after all. But then I thought that even if it seems a little unnecessary to me, perhaps having a bit of my personal voice will make this blog a more engaging read. So, welcome! Let me start by explaining who and what this blog is for.

This blog is for me because writing posts will force me to better research and articulate my ideas. I intend to post primarily technical content, and perhaps the occasional bit of career advice or analysis of current events. This blog is where I will keep all my writing, and where I will direct anyone who asks, who are you and what do you know? Plus, I'll get to produce something concrete with the time I spend on my hobbies.

On the other hand, this blog is for you as well. I would be delighted if you were to learn something or think of a new idea in exchange for your time spent here. I hope I can make it easier to be an independent seeker of knowledge. I will do my best to continuously improve the quality of my content, so please excuse any amateurish writing, and feel free to point out mistakes.

Happy reading,
E. Wang
